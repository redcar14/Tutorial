Introduction

As (maybe) everyone knows, Mario Kart is a racing game where you play as your favorite Mario character and race to win first place. There are 8 cups in Mario Kart you must take on to complete the game and four different speed classes. 50cc's, 100cc's, 150cc's, and Mirror. For the cups,theres the Mushroom Cup, the Flower Cup, the Star Cup, the Special Cup, the Shell Cup, the Banana Cup, the Leaf Cup, and finally, the Lightning Cup.
